Chicken Satay

Delicious and easy to make Chicken Satay

I made this dish last night and couldn't be more pleased. The marinade was only a handful of ingredients, and the skewers only needed a couple of minutes on the grill.

While the chicken was marinating, I went ahead and made a simple spaghetti with peanut sauce and a fresh cucumber salad. All told, this recipe was only about 20 minutes of cooking.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts, Thinly sliced
  • 1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Cup Oil
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 2 Cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients to marinade for 1 hour or up to over night
  2. Add chicken to skewers, folding over so that the strips stay on
  3. Grill on medium high heat, turning skewers every 3-4 minutes
